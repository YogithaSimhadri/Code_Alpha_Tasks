# Task 3: Data Visualization Dashboard - Tableau Edition

## 1. Tool Selection
For this task, **Tableau Public** was used to build high-fidelity data visualizations directly from our scraped Flipkart laptop dataset containing Laptop Names and Selling Prices.

## 2. Visualizations Created

### A. Laptop Price Comparison Matrix (Bar Chart)
This chart displays a direct comparison of individual laptop models against their active market selling prices on Flipkart.
![Laptop Prices](laptop_prices_bar_chart.png)

### B. Retail Price Distribution Index (Histogram)
This visualization groups the laptops into pricing brackets to show the density of inventory available across budget, mid-range, and premium tiers.
![Price Distribution](price_distribution_histogram.png)

## 3. Key Analytical Insights
1. **Pricing Density:** The histogram reveals that the highest concentration of laptops listed on Flipkart falls within the budget to mid-range tier, showing the platform's focus on general consumers.
2. **Premium Outliers:** A select few specialized laptop configurations sit far to the right of the distribution, representing premium performance models.
